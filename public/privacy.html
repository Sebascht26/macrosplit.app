We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website macrosplit.app.

1. General Information

You can use our website without providing personal data. We only collect personal data when you voluntarily provide it (for example, by contacting us via email). This data will not be shared without your consent.

2. Use of Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense, a service provided by Google Inc. (“Google”), to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses “cookies,” small text files stored on your device, and “web beacons” (invisible graphics) to help analyze website usage and display targeted ads.

Information generated by cookies and web beacons about your use of this website (including your IP address) is transmitted to a Google server in the USA and stored there. Google may share this information with third parties if required by law or if third parties process the data on Google’s behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with other data held by Google.

3. Cookies and Consent

We use a cookie consent banner to comply with GDPR. You can choose to accept or reject cookies used for personalized advertising. If you reject cookies, you may still see ads, but they will not be personalized.

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You can also object to the processing of your data at any time. Please contact us at the email provided in the Impressum.
